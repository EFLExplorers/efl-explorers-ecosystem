# Teacher Platform Manifest

- **Role:** Classroom management and PPP (Presentation, Practice, Production) workflows.
- **Tech Stack:** Next.js App Router, Prisma Client
- **Database Scope:** Primarily `TeacherUserMapping`, `Student`, `StudentUserMapping`, `User`, `Lesson`, `Task`, `Material`, `Message`, `Announcement`, and `Event` (see `schema.prisma`).
