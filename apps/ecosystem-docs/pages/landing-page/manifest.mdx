# Landing Page Manifest

- **Role:** Marketing, SEO, lead generation, and public-facing entry to the product.
- **Tech Stack:** Next.js App Router, Prisma Client
- **Database Scope:** Primarily `Page`, `ContentItem`, `PageSection`, `SiteSection`, `RouteSection`, `ContentRelationship`, `MediaAsset`, `Faq`, `TeamMember`, `AboutStat`, `CoreValue`, `LessonModule`, `TeacherBenefit`, `Testimonial`, and auth-related models such as `User`, `Account`, `Session`, `SsoToken` (see `schema.prisma`).
