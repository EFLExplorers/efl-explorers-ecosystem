# The "No Assumptions" Doctrine

This document outlines the strict operational doctrine for the EFL Explorers monorepo.

## 1. No Assumptions

If you do not see the file, path, variable, or logic flow in your current context, you **MUST** ask. Do not invent boilerplate or guess at implementation details.

## 2. Planning First

Before generating code, provide a `<PLANNING>` block. List your assumptions there for approval.

## 3. Strict Types

- Do not use `any`.
- Use **Zod** for all form validation (including subscription and lead flows).
