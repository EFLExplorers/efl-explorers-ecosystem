# Curriculum Platform Manifest

- **Role:** Cambridge-aligned curriculum structure, authoring, and published educational content.
- **Tech Stack:** Next.js App Router, Prisma Client
- **Database Scope:** Primarily `Curriculum`, `CurriculumProgram`, `CurriculumLevel`, `CurriculumUnit`, `CurriculumPublishSnapshot`, `CurriculumPublishSnapshotUnit`, `CurriculumManager`, `CurriculumInvite`, `Lesson`, `LessonModule`, `Material`, and `LessonMaterial` (see `schema.prisma`).
